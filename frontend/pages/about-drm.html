About DRM

DRM (Digital Radio Mondiale) is a digital radio transmission standard used for long wave, medium wave, and short wave broadcasting.

What is DRM?

DRM offers several advantages over traditional analog radio broadcasting, including high-quality sound, data transmission capabilities, and better spectrum efficiency.
